About Us

Our Menus

The clove club is a relaxed fine dining restaurant set in the historic rooms of the Grade II-listed shoreditch town hall, london. Led by chef-patron isaac mchale, we serve a seasonal tasting menu featuring modern, elegant dishes rooted in technique but stripped back to their essential elements. We place a great emphasis on sourcing the best produce the british isles has to offer, showcased in dishes inspired by isaac’s culinary memories and travels.

Open since 2013

The clove club currently holds two michelin stars. It has won a raft of awards, including the highest new entry to the prestigious world’s 50 best restaurant list in 2016, where it has been the highest-placed UK restaurant to this day; No.1 Restaurant in the UK in the sunday times top 100; winner of the service award and fifth place in the uk national restaurant awards; bmw squaremeal london restaurant of the year; and london restaurant festival amex restaurant of the year.

To accompany our tasting menus, we offer an extensive wine list that showcases some of the most exciting contemporary winemakers alongside a selection of fine, classical old world wines.

Our aim is to create dining experiences that both excite and surprise the senses, serving delicious food that lives long in our guests' memories. We look forward to welcoming you to the clove club.
